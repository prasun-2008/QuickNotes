About NEB Notes

Founded by passionate student developers Prasun Tripathi & Pranshu Tripathi, NEB Notes simplifies learning for Class 12 NEB students across Nepal.

Our mission is to provide organized, syllabus-based, and easy-to-understand notes and model questions.

From beautiful design to practical functionality, every feature is crafted with NEB students in mind to help them study better, faster, and with more confidence.
