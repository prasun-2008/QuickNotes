About QuickNotes

QuickNotes is a dedicated platform developed by two ambitious young developers, Prasun Tripathi and Prabhas Budathoki, who are passionate about digitalizing and simplifying the learning experience for Grade 12 NEB Computer Science students across Nepal.

Our mission is to provide students with organized, high-quality notes and question sets that are easy to access and use, making studying efficient and effective.

By leveraging modern web technologies and user-centric design, we strive to empower Nepali students to excel academically and prepare confidently for their exams.

Our Story

Founded by Prasun Tripathi (17) and Prabhas Budathoki (16), QuickNotes was born out of their shared vision to make computer science notes and question sets more accessible for their fellow Nepali students.
